# Repeatable customer rollout

This document defines how Aegis turns a bounded pilot into a repeatable customer
rollout motion.

## Required rollout surfaces

- post-pilot cohort definitions
- deployment-track-specific launch handoffs
- bounded expansion stages
- committed rollout evidence and rollback references

## Constraint

Customer rollout may not expand through bespoke founder judgment. Each cohort must
stay bound to committed deployment tracks, handoff docs, and rollback-aware runbooks.
