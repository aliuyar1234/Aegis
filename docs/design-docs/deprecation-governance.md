# Deprecation Governance

This document defines the PHASE-20 deprecation and sunset surface for Aegis.

## Why deprecation matters here

By PHASE-20, Aegis has certified packs, starter kits, deployment tracks, and adoption
programs.
If those surfaces change without explicit sunset rules, the system becomes harder to
operate safely than it is to build.

## What must be explicit

- what asset kind is being sunset
- the bounded asset identifiers affected
- the notice window
- the successor or migration references
- freeze actions during the sunset window
- the rollback or suspension runbook

## Non-goals

- no marketing announcement machinery
- no promise that every surface remains supported forever
- no deprecation path that bypasses certified evidence and rollback discipline
