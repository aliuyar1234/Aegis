# Rollout cohort admission

Use this runbook when admitting a post-pilot customer cohort into the committed
rollout program.

## Workflow

1. open `meta/repeatable-customer-rollout.yaml`
2. confirm the cohort still maps to a committed deployment track and predecessor pilot wave
3. verify the required evidence inputs and handoff docs are current
4. attach the cohort review to the GA transition surface before expansion

## Do not do this

- do not admit a cohort outside the committed rollout stages
- do not bypass rollout evidence or rollback references
