# Customer Incident Review

Use this runbook when preparing a PHASE-20 incident-review packet for customer or
internal handoff.

## Inputs

- the incident-review packet in `meta/incident-review-packets.yaml`
- the packet's source exercise or incident class
- the referenced evidence bundles and runbooks

## Steps

1. Identify the correct packet for the incident class and deployment context.
2. Collect the required evidence references before drafting conclusions.
3. Review the response runbooks used during the incident.
4. Confirm the follow-up references that should absorb the learning.
5. Keep the review bounded to committed evidence instead of speculative narrative.

## Exit condition

The incident review is ready when a reader can reproduce the basis for the handoff
from the committed evidence and runbook set alone.
