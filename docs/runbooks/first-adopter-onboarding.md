# First adopter onboarding

Use this runbook when guiding a new team through a supported Aegis adoption track.

## Preconditions

- the target adoption profile is selected
- the reference deployment track is selected
- required evidence bundles are available
- required operator exercises are scheduled or completed

## Workflow

1. select the adoption profile and confirm the matching deployment track
2. confirm the starter kit, sandbox profiles, and governing policy bundle
3. verify the required phase evidence bundles are current
4. walk the operator exercise list and runbooks
5. capture the generated adoption evidence bundle for the onboarding packet

## Do not do this

- do not invent an unsupported topology during onboarding
- do not bypass starter-kit policy or sandbox bindings
- do not treat public compatibility publication as a substitute for operator drills
