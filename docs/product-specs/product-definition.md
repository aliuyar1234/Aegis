# Product definition

## One-line definition

Aegis Runtime is a durable, BEAM-native control plane for long-lived AI sessions that coordinate tools, browsers, models, and humans under real-world failure.

## Core users

- platform engineers building AI execution systems
- product teams shipping high-value service automation
- operators who need runtime visibility and intervention

## Product center of gravity

The product center of gravity is **runtime credibility**, not prompt cleverness.

Aegis wins when it can make the following true:
- a session survives failure
- the operator understands what happened
- policy and approvals are explicit
- recovery does not lie
- browser work can be controlled and replayed
- the architecture remains legible across languages

## What customers should feel

- “This is infrastructure, not a toy.”
- “I can trust this in a live operational workflow.”
- “I can see what the system is doing and step in if needed.”
- “This would be hard to rebuild correctly from scratch.”

## Product anti-goals

- replacing every enterprise workflow product
- being the prettiest no-code tool
- optimizing for a 2-day MVP
- maximizing model novelty
- hiding complexity behind magic language
